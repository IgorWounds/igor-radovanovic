About Me

A Software Engineering and AI automation expert with a passion for building scalable and efficient systems.

My journey into technology wasn't a traditional one. It began with a degree in Psychology, which gave me a unique lens for problem-solving. It taught me to look beyond the code and data to understand the human-side of every technical challenge. This foundation allows me to bridge the gap between complex systems and the people they're built to serve.

As a leader, I'm passionate about fostering autonomous, collaborative teams and building solutions that are not just technically robust, but also elegant and meaningful.

When I'm not architecting data pipelines or fine-tuning models, you'll likely find me on the mats training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. A discipline that mirrors engineering in its blend of strategy, leverage, and continuous problem-solving.

Otherwise, I'm probably lost in a good book, exploring everything from ancient philosophy to modern AI theory. I believe the best innovations come from connecting diverse ideas, whether they're found in a deep learning paper or a philosophical text.
